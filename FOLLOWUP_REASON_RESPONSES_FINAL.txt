Follow-up Reason Responses - Context-Aware Messaging

## Purpose
Provide tailored, empathetic responses based on the specific follow-up reason the user selects. Each reason requires different tone and messaging.

---

## 1. Scheduled Follow-up

**User Selection:** "Scheduled follow-up" (Doctor asked patient to return)

**Context:** This is a planned, routine follow-up as instructed by the doctor. The patient is returning as expected, not due to worsening symptoms.

**Response Template:**
```
"Got it. Any updates you'd like to share with the doctor? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
```

**Alternative Variations:**
- "Perfect! Any updates you'd like to share with the doctor? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "Understood. Would you like to share any updates since your last visit? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "Got it. Any changes in your condition the doctor should know about? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."

**Tone:**
- Routine, matter-of-fact
- Not overly concerned
- Optional/casual about notes

**Storage:**
```json
{
  "followup_reason": "scheduled",
  "followup_context": "Planned follow-up as instructed by doctor"
}
```

**Next Step:** Ask for followup_notes (optional, can skip)

**Component:** Free text input with prominent "Skip" button

---

## 2. New Concern

**User Selection:** "New concern" (Different issue than last visit)

**Context:** Patient is returning but for a DIFFERENT health issue than the previous visit. This is not about the original condition.

**Response Template:**
```
"What new symptoms or changes have you noticed? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
```

**Alternative Variations:**
- "I see, you have a different concern this time. What new symptoms or changes have you noticed? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "Understood. Could you tell me about the new symptoms? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "Got it. What new symptoms or changes have you noticed? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."

**Tone:**
- Attentive, open
- Slightly more inquisitive (it's a new issue)
- Still optional, not pushy

**Storage:**
```json
{
  "followup_reason": "new_concern",
  "followup_context": "Patient has different/new concern unrelated to previous visit"
}
```

**Next Step:** Ask for followup_notes (optional, can skip)

**Component:** Free text input with "Skip" option

**Note:** Doctor may need more context since this isn't about the original condition they treated

---

## 3. Ongoing Issue

**User Selection:** "Ongoing issue" (Same problem, not better)

**Context:** The original condition has NOT improved or has worsened. Patient is concerned that treatment didn't work or symptoms persist.

**Response Template:**
```
"I'm sorry to hear that. Can you describe what's still bothering you? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
```

**Alternative Variations:**
- "I understand, the previous issue is still bothering you. Can you describe what's still bothering you? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "Got it. How have your symptoms been since the last visit? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."
- "I see. What symptoms are continuing? This will help the doctor prepare for your visit. You can also skip this."

**Tone:**
- Empathetic, concerned
- Acknowledges patient's frustration/discomfort
- Gentle, supportive

**Storage:**
```json
{
  "followup_reason": "ongoing_issue",
  "followup_context": "Original condition persisting or not improving as expected"
}
```

**Next Step:** Ask for followup_notes (optional, can skip)

**Component:** Free text input with "Skip" option

**Note:** This requires the most empathy - patient may be frustrated that treatment didn't work

---

## After Notes Provided or Skipped

### If User Provides Notes

**User Input:** *Any text describing their condition/updates*

**Response Template:**
```
"Thanks for sharing that. The doctor will review this before your appointment."
```

**Alternative Variations:**
- "Thank you. The doctor will have this information ready for your visit."
- "Got it, thank you. This will help the doctor prepare for your appointment."
- "Perfect, the doctor will review these details before you meet."

**Tone:**
- Appreciative
- Reassuring (doctor will be prepared)
- Brief, move forward

**Storage:**
```json
{
  "followup_notes": "user's text input",
  "notes_provided": true
}
```

**Next Step:** Show previous_doctors component

---

### If User Skips

**User Action:** Clicks "Skip" or says "no updates" / "skip" / "nothing to add"

**Response Template:**
```
"No problem. Would you like to book with {doctor_name} again? They have your medical history from your last visit."
```

**Alternative Variations:**
- "Okay, we can skip that. Would you like to book with {doctor_name} again?"
- "No worries. Shall we book with {doctor_name} who saw you last time?"
- "That's fine. Would you prefer to see {doctor_name} again?"

**Tone:**
- Accepting, non-judgmental
- Smooth transition to next step
- No dwelling on the skip

**Storage:**
```json
{
  "followup_notes": null,
  "notes_provided": false,
  "notes_skipped": true
}
```

**Next Step:** Show previous_doctors component

---

## Transitioning to Previous Doctors

After notes (provided or skipped), always show previous doctors component.

**Primary Doctor Available:**
```
"Would you like to book with {doctor_name} again? They have your medical history and context from your last visit on {formatted_date}."
```

**Multiple Previous Doctors:**
```
"Here are the doctors you've seen recently. Would you like to book with any of them?"
```

**No Previous Doctor Found:**
```
"Let me show you our available doctors."
```

**Component:** `previous_doctors` with:
- Primary doctor highlighted at top
- Shows last visit date
- Shows next 3 available slots for quick booking
- "See other doctors" option at bottom
